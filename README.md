üõ†Ô∏è Technical Specifications
Based on our research and procurement reports, the drone uses the following core components:

1. Flight Control & Communication
Flight Controller: APM 2.8 (Arduino compatible) with integrated 3-axis gyro, accelerometer, magnetometer, and barometer.
Remote Radio: FlySky FS-i6 2.4G 10CH Transmitter, featuring AFHDS 2A technology for long-distance control.
2. Propulsion System
Motors: DJI 2212 920KV Brushless DC (BLDC) motors. These were selected for their high torque and efficiency, supporting a max thrust of 1200g.
Propellers: 10x4.5 Self-locking propellers (Fixed pitch).
ESC: 30A Electronic Speed Controllers to regulate motor speed and direction.
3. Power & Frame
Frame: S500 Quadcopter frame with carbon fiber landing gear.
Battery: 2200mAh Tiger 3S 11.1V 35C Lipo Battery.
